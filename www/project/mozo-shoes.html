Mozo Shoes

I was contacted by Twin Technologies to participate and lead a small team to do a complete front-end overhaul of MozoShoes.com.

Web

This project was challenging in that we had the contraints of building the site on the Demandware platform which has a workflow built around the Eclipse IDE. Running into walls with Eclipse syncing in OS X, we developed a modern Sass and JS-building workflow with Gulp, gulp-shell, and sycning files to the Demandware instance via Transmit WebDAV.

The Gulp workflow enabled us to tackle the site design, moving Mozo away from Bootstrap and toward a modern Sass front-end utilizing Susy and other custom mixins.

The project proved to be a success and the Mozo team was stoked about the fresh new site, the new visuals, and a step forward for their brand and eCommerce site.
